Where have all the bees gone?!

Spring has sprung - birds are chirping, flowers are in bloom, and buzz of the bees...is nowhere to be heard!

The Queen Bees of Beehive Lane have lost their bees. Without the scout bees, there will be no one to find new sources of nectar and pollen. Without pollination, there goes human food crops. Without the soldier bees, there’s no way to protect the hive. The Queen Bees and the human population are doomed unless they can get their worker bees back! Help your Queen Bee restore her hive, but “bee” careful - the journey is long and danger abounds.

Join the mission to save the honeybees!

FAQ

Beeline is a family-friendly board game for 2-6 players that offers unique fast-paced, competitive, and unpredictable gameplay for the whole family.

After an unsatisfying board game experience with her husband and stepson, co-creator Kim declared that she needed to design a board game of her own. Understanding the need to get more people talking about the bee plight and the desire to help the cause, Beeline was born. With the help of her husband, an initial prototype was developed, play-tested, and entered into Hasbro's Next Best Board Game contest. It wasn't selected for the semi-finals, but the duo was not deterred because they saw, after several rounds of play-test with game enthusiasts, genuine enjoyment and demand for the game. The beauty of the digital age is: you don't need a big company to pursue passion projects!

Bees are the perfect theme for a game intended to bring people together. Did you know that honeybees are very much like humans? It's true! They're highly social creatures that work together to keep the hive running and copacetic. Sadly, not much is known about the honeybee plight and, because they are facing a pandemic, we thought this would be a great way to increase awareness by incorporating useful information and donating to the cause.

The game can accommodate anywhere from 2 to 6 players. And the beauty about Beeline is that anyone can play -- parents with children, aunts/uncles with nieces and nephews, friends. Beeline offers entertainment (and education) for everyone. Recommended Ages: 7+.

A full game runs around 15 minutes, so perfect for multiple rematches. Game instructions are easy to learn, just a few simple rules and you're good to go!
